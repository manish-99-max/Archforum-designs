About Us

Where Architecture
Meets Craft

Archforum Designs is a full-service architecture and interior design studio based in Golaghat, Assam. We believe great design is more than aesthetics — it's about creating spaces that feel genuinely alive, functional and personal to the people who inhabit them.

From concept sketches to final execution, our team handles every detail of your project with precision and passion. Residential homes, commercial interiors, institutional buildings — we bring the same level of dedication to every brief we take on.
